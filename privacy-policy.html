Privacy Policy

At TalentApply, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect/p>

We collect personal and non-personal information to provide and improve our services. This information may include:

Personal Information

Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details.

Resume/CV information, such as work experience, education, and skills.

Account login credentials.

Not use our platform for any illegal or unauthorized purposes, including but not limited to scraping, data mining, or reselling our services.

Usage Data

IP address, browser type, device type, and usage patterns on our platform.

Job search behavior, such as keywords, locations, and interactions with job postings.

Data collected via cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies.

Payment Information

Credit/debit card details or other payment information provided for subscriptions or purchases.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and enhance our platform and services. Specifically, we use your data for:

Service Delivery

To personalize and automate your job search experience.

To provide resume analysis, interview preparation, and career resources.

To communicate with you regarding updates, promotions, and customer support.

Improving Our Platform

To analyze usage trends and improve user experience.

To test and develop new features, services, and tools.

Marketing and Communication

With your consent, we may send you promotional emails, newsletters, or updates relevant to your job search.

You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.

Payment Processing

To process payments for premium services or subscriptions.

We use trusted third-party payment processors and do not store your payment information.

3. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on TalentApply. Cookies help us remember your preferences, improve site functionality, and provide you with personalized content.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Necessary for the proper functioning of the platform.

To provide resume analysis, interview preparation, and career resources.

Performance Cookies: Help us understand how you use the platform to improve performance.

Marketing Cookies: Enable us to provide relevant job opportunities and career content.

Improving Our Platform

Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may limit your use of certain features on the platform.

5. International Users

TalentApply operates globally, and your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of your own. We will take necessary steps to ensure that your personal data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy, regardless of location.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, practices, or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy regularly. Your continued use of TalentApply constitutes acceptance of any updates.
